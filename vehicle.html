The Vehicle.

The best thing about overlanding is the diverse set of vehicles you can bring. It can range from an old family wagon to a heavily modified 4x4 truck. The vehicle has a special place in the owner's heart because it is treated as one of the companions in their journey.

Common Types >>

Old/Restored

Toyota Land Cruiser (1986)

Many people choose this type of vehicle because it is cheap and reliable. These vehicles are more rugged and have less features that can break.

Family SUV

Subaru Forester

These vehicles can be used both as a daily driver and as an overlander. It provides everyday comfort while still providing enough offroading capabilities.

Sports/Wagon

Audi A4

This is a more unique type of vehicle for those who want to have fun and stand out. These vehicles have a lower center of gravity and are similar to rally car builds.

Modified 4x4

Toyota FJ Cruiser

This is one of the most common type of overlander. Most of its modifications are specialized for overlanding. It is one of the most capable off road, however, it is not an efficient daily driver.
